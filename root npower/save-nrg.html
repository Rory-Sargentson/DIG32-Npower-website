Couldn't find your elegibility?


No worries, here is an intelligent list of ways you can save energy at home.

Energy Efficient Light Bulbs

When possible, change all your light bulbs to Halogen, which will reduce energy usage and will add 15% more brightness to your home.


Minimal Appliance Usage

Cutting down on using energy hungry appliances will help reduce monthly costs. Try making your cuppa by using gas instead of eletricity.


Limit Gaming Time

Games chew up a lot of energy, especially online gaming. We reccomend going outside to play and laying off Fortnite for a Fortnight.

Sun & Solar

Harvest the sun's powerful rays to dry your clothes, grow your plants and charge your house or smart devices through solar collection.


Unplug Unsused Utilities

All appliances have a 'soft on' when plugged in, meaning they will still draw power from time to time.


Behavior & Attitude

Personal choices can effect how you conserve energy and save money in the long run.
